Important Medicinal Plants in Southern Tagalog, Conservation and Mass Production for Development and commercialization of New Products

Category: Farming

ADD TO COLLECTION

ABSTRACT

The ultimate goal of the research is to develop special products from important medicinal plant in Southern Tagalog. The research started with identifying the traditional and alternative health care practitioners, and eventually the plants they consider as the most useful for common ailments. From this nine quality products were developed - native guava jam and soap, coconut jam, tamarind multi-purpose concentrate, calamansi soap, native ginger massage cream and tea, and turmeric natural healing cream and tea. The products were then tested for acceptability, and community nurseries and plantations were established as sources of raw materials.

AWARD

Best New Product Award Turmeric Natural Healing Cream and Tea), DA-BAR's 8th Agriculture and Fisheries Technology Forum Exhibition, SM Mega Trade Hall, Mandaluyong City, August 2012.

BENEFICIARIES

The technologies development shared with any farmers group, investor, entrepreneur or other interested individuals.

TECHNOLOGY STATUS

The production of raw materials being carried out by farmers, while the processing is performed by Green Rescue Association (GRO) through the complaint facility. In terms of marketing, there are now shops and other products are made available. Furthermore, an exclusive contract was recently entered in GRO with Bizpaye, Philippines, Inc., for the promotion, sales, and marketing of the products.
